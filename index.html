ABOUT ME

I am Blesson JF, a Computer Science & Engineering student from Trivandrum with a deep-seated passion for the intersection of design and technology. While my academic background is in B.Tech CSE, my heart lies in the creative process of web and UI/UX design. I believe that a great application should not only function seamlessly but also inspire through its visual language. Beyond the screen, I find balance in music production, playing instruments, and digital art—creative pursuits that constantly inform my design perspective.
